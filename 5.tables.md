## 5.2. Toxicity Scale

Roll twice, once in Column A and once in Column B. Reroll if both results are neutral, otherwise you aren't dealing with a toxin.

### Table 5.2: Toxicity Scale

|A: Antidote Strength|d6|B: Toxin Strength|
|--------------------|--|-----------------|
|Neutral|1-2|Neutral|
|Mild|3|Mild|
|Moderate|4|Moderate|
|Good|5|Severe|
|Strong|6|Fatal|
